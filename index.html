About

I am a 3rd year PhD student in the CSE department of OSU, advised by Tamal K. Dey. My research interest is generally Computational Topology. I am especially interested in the theoretical foundation of Topological Data Analysis.

I received a B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology, China, and a M.E. degree in Software Engineering from Tsinghua University, China. My master's thesis is concerning on-the-fly simplification and adaptive extraction of iso-surfaces from volume datasets.

After I finished my Master's degree, I went to work for 2 companies, Baidu and VMware.
