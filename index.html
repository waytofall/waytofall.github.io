About

I am currently a 1st year PhD student in the CSE department of OSU. I am now looking for research advisor. My research interest generally lies in using Computer Science as well as Mathematical knowledge to design Algorithms. I have particular interests in Mathematics, due to its power in defining, modeling, and solving problems in a rigorous and concise way.

I received a B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology, China, and a M.E. degree in Software Engineering from Tsinghua University, China. My master's thesis is concerning on-the-fly simplification and adaptive extraction of iso-surfaces from volume datasets.

After I finished my Master's degree, I went to work for 2 companies, Baidu and VMware, both in Beijing, China. My responsibilities for these 2 jobs respectively are maintaining and developing of the online-search module for the special search result and the PCoIP protocol for remote desktop. I especially appreciate my work experience in VMware, where I met fabulous colleagues and managers and had a happy time working/team building with them.
